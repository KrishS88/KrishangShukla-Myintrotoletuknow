Constant time, Different worlds.

Hello, I'm Krish! I like to think of my life as different chapters. I have spent portions of my life in different ends of the globe, each with a different mindset. Navigate each slide to begin travelling with me!
We start in the tranquil suburbs of Ann Arbor, Michigan. Growing up here was beautiful, yet uneventful. I spent time building my love for comics, animations, and - like many Michigan folks - cars. I was more reserved, but it's a place that let my imagination fly. That was, until the age of seven.
Welcome to the Bay Area, California. Life turns upside down, or maybe downside up. For the next eleven years, my social skills skyrocket, my musical background flourishes, and I expand on my extracurriculars, yet I wasn't sure what I wanted to pursue. I was eager to find out as I entered high school...
...in India? We moved here for my father's work experience. This meant education in a new environment and culture. However, Bangalore was the shortest yet most significant segment. From building my professional outlook, to completing community projects, to even opening my heart towards media development.
Our final stop is in Atlanta. I am now a Computational Media student with many perspectives. I'm adaptable to rapid changes, I'm getting myself in campus involvements, and I am one step closer to applying my skills to the world. I would not be where I am today without experiencing these chapters along with the endless support from my loving family.
